Superheros

On this page, I will describe 5 different superheros and their Names, Aliases, Images, Superpowers, and Weaknesses.

Name Alias Image Superpower Weakness
Superman Clark Kent Has super strength, has the ability to fly, has the ability to shoot lasers from his eyes, and most importantly, has the ability to cause massive destruction to a city while trying to defeat the villain Kryptonite
Saitama One Punch Man, Caped Baldy Has tremendous, seemingly infinite amount of strength and speed. Nobody comes close to his level of strength, and his ideals of heroism lead him to absolve himself of credit to many of his amazing accomplishments Boredom, nothing else can defeat him
Mob White T Poison Has incredible psychic powers, above just about everyone he's come across. He's a good kid, and his morals limit his use of his power only for when he or a loved one is in danger His emotional state continues to build up until he releases a large amount of power, but afterwards becomes really tired and weak
Batman Bruce Wayne Has lots of money, which allows him to develop advanced tools for fighting crime. He also has to be physically fit in order to utilize his tools and take down the bad guys He's just a regular guy, so I guess you just have to get to him to defeat him, he's also vulnerable if his tools are rendered useless
Jeff Bezos Rich Guy Has lots of money and owned massive corporation Divorce
